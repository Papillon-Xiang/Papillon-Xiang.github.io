About

Hi! I am a current Ph.D. student in Applied Mathematics and Statistics at Johns Hopkins University, supervised by Prof. Yanxun Xu. I completed my Master's degree in AMS at Johns Hopkins University and my Bachelor's degree in Applied Mathematics at Xi'an Jiaotong-Liverpool University.

My research interests lie in developing interpretable and data-driven models for decision-making in complex systems through the lens of statistics and machine learning. I am interested in developing interpretable models to quantify causal effects, forecast outcomes, and optimize strategies in complex systems such as healthcare.
